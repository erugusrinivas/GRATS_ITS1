About Us

GRATS IT Services was founded with a simple motive of offering the clients exactly what they want, how they want and when they want. By leveraging for its clients its technological edge and right-sourcing advantage, GRATS in a short period of time has grown to become one of the most trusted strategic technology partners. Treating every client as the top priority, we customize our solutions and services to align with the unique needs of each client.

Headquartered in Marathahalli, Bangalore, GRATS employs a unique global delivery platform to minister its offerings spanning from application development and maintenance to business process reengineering.
